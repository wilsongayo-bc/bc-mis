# User Email Verification (Link + 6-Digit Code)

This guide explains how to verify your email address in Coldea MIS. A verified email is required for self-service password reset and helps secure your account.

## What You Need
- Access to your inbox for the email address saved on your profile
- The verification email (contains a button/link and a 6-digit code)

## When Verification Is Required
- After your account is created and your email is not yet verified
- After you update/change your email address (verification resets and must be done again)

## Step 1 — Check Your Email Verification Status
1. Login to Coldea MIS.
2. Open **Profile**.
3. Look for the **Email Verification** status.

Screenshot placeholder:
- [Screenshot: Profile page showing “Email not verified” status]

## Step 2 — Send (or Resend) Verification Email
1. On **Profile**, click **Send verification email** (or **Resend**).
2. Wait for the email to arrive.

Notes:
- The resend button has a short cooldown to prevent spam.
- If you don’t receive an email, check Spam/Junk folder.

Screenshot placeholder:
- [Screenshot: Profile page “Send verification email” button + cooldown timer]

## Step 3 — Open the Verification Email
Look for an email with a subject similar to **Email Verification - Benedict College**.

The email includes:
- A **Verify Email** button/link (opens the verification page)
- A **6-digit verification code**

Screenshot placeholders:
- [Screenshot: Email body with Verify Email button]
- [Screenshot: Email body showing 6-digit code]

## Step 4 — Verify Using the Code
1. Click the **Verify Email** button in the email.
2. The link opens the verification page.
3. Enter the **6-digit verification code**.
4. Click **Verify**.

- [Screenshot: Verify Email page with code input]

## Step 5 — Confirm Verification
After a successful verification:
- The page shows **Email verified**.
- Your Profile should show your email as verified.

- [Screenshot: Success message “Email verified”]

## Common Issues

### “Missing verification token”
- You opened the verification page directly without using the emailed link.
- Use the link from the email and try again.

### Code rejected / invalid code
- Ensure you typed the code exactly (6 digits).
- If the code expired, request a new verification email from **Profile**.

### No verification email received
- Check Spam/Junk folder.
- Confirm your profile email is correct.
- Try resending after the cooldown.
