## Overview
Coldea MIS uses email verification to confirm that you have access to the email address saved on your profile.

Verified email is required for:
- Password Reset (Forgot Password)

## Step 1 — Check your verification status
1. Login.
2. Open **Profile**.
3. Look for your email verification status.

## Step 2 — Send verification email
1. On **Profile**, click **Send verification email** (or **Resend**).
2. Wait for the verification email to arrive.

Notes:
- The resend action has a short cooldown.
- Check Spam/Junk if you don’t see the email.

## Step 3 — Open the verification email
The email includes:
- A **Verify Email** button/link
- A **6-digit verification code**

## Step 4 — Verify your email
1. Click the email’s **Verify Email** button/link (opens the verification page).
2. Enter the 6-digit code.
3. Click **Verify**.

## Step 5 — Confirm verification
After success:
- The verification page shows a success message.
- Your Profile should show **Email verified**.

## Common issues
### Missing verification token
- The verification page must be opened using the emailed link.

### No email received
- Check Spam/Junk folder.
- Confirm the email saved in your Profile is correct.
- Use **Resend** after the cooldown.

### Email changed
- Changing your email requires verification again. Send a new verification email from **Profile**.
