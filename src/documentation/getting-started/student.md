# Welcome, Student! 🎓

As a Student in the Coldea Management Information System, you have access to view your academic information, track your progress, and manage your educational journey. This guide will help you navigate the system and stay on top of your academic responsibilities.

## 🎯 Your Role Overview

**Access Level**: Student Access  
**Key Responsibilities**: Monitor academic progress, manage personal information, track payments, and access educational resources

### What You Can Access:
- ✅ **Dashboard**: Your personal academic overview
- ✅ **My Profile**: Personal information and academic records
- ✅ **Courses**: View courses you're enrolled in
- ✅ **Subjects**: Access subjects within your courses
- ✅ **Enrollments**: View your enrollment history and status
- ✅ **Payments**: Track your payment history and outstanding balances
- ✅ **Books**: Access library resources and borrowing history
- ✅ **Schedules**: View your class schedule
- ✅ **Timetable**: Visual view of your weekly schedule

### What You Cannot Access:
- ❌ Other students' information
- ❌ Administrative functions
- ❌ System settings
- ❌ Financial management tools
- ❌ User management

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Complete Your Student Profile
1. Click on **"My Profile"** in the sidebar
2. Verify and update your personal information:
   - Contact details (phone, email)
   - Emergency contact information
   - Address information
3. Upload a current profile photo
4. Review your academic information

### Step 2: Explore Your Dashboard
Your student dashboard provides:
- **Academic Overview**: Current semester information
- **Course Summary**: Courses you're enrolled in
- **Payment Status**: Outstanding balances and payment history
- **Recent Activities**: Latest enrollments and updates
- **Quick Actions**: Common tasks you can perform

### Step 3: Review Your Enrollments
1. Navigate to **Enrollments** to see your course history
2. Check your current semester enrollments
3. Review past semester records
4. Verify enrollment status for each course
