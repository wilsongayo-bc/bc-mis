# Welcome, Teacher! 👨‍🏫👩‍🏫

As a Teacher in the Coldea Management Information System, you have access to academic management tools that help you effectively manage your courses, students, and teaching responsibilities. This guide will help you navigate the system and accomplish your daily tasks efficiently.

## 🎯 Your Role Overview

**Access Level**: Academic Access  
**Key Responsibilities**: Course management, student instruction, schedule coordination, and academic assessment

### What You Can Access:
- ✅ **Dashboard**: Academic overview with your teaching metrics
- ✅ **My Profile**: Personal information and teaching credentials
- ✅ **Students**: View and manage students in your courses
- ✅ **Courses**: Manage courses you're assigned to teach
- ✅ **Subjects**: Access subjects within your courses
- ✅ **Enrollments**: View student enrollments in your classes
- ✅ **Books**: Access library resources for your courses
- ✅ **Schedules**: Manage your teaching schedule
- ✅ **Timetable**: View and coordinate class schedules

### What You Cannot Access:
- ❌ User management (except viewing student profiles)
- ❌ Financial/payment information
- ❌ System settings and configuration
- ❌ Administrative reports

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Complete Your Teaching Profile
1. Click on **"My Profile"** in the sidebar
2. Update your academic credentials and qualifications
3. Add your teaching specializations
4. Upload a professional profile photo
5. Set your office hours and contact preferences

### Step 2: Review Your Dashboard
Your teacher dashboard shows:
- **Your Courses**: List of courses you're teaching this semester
- **Student Count**: Total students across all your courses
- **Schedule Overview**: Today's classes and upcoming sessions
- **Recent Activities**: Latest student enrollments and course updates

### Step 3: Explore Your Courses
1. Navigate to **Courses** to see your assigned courses
2. Click on each course to view details
3. Review course descriptions and learning objectives
4. Check enrolled student lists
